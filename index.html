Studio Overview

HackerManHub (HMH) is a Roblox development studio committed to creating immersive and fun experiences on the Roblox platform. Currently, HMH is working on two upcoming games: Echos Of Admins (EOA) and Just Another Tuesday (JAT). Although the games are not yet released, this wiki will serve as an information hub for the studio, its developers, and the individual game projects.

The studio focuses on high‑quality gameplay, creative design, and community engagement. Stay tuned for updates on the releases!
